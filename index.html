FAQ

Questions teams ask before moving to RocketPSA

Is RocketPSA only for MSPs?
RocketPSA is MSP-first, but the platform also supports school departments that need structured request intake, onboarding, event coordination, and contact visibility.
What does the platform include in the core workspace?
The core workspace centers on dashboards, tickets, projects, client records, invoicing, and calendar visibility.
Can we manage leads and sales inside RocketPSA?
Yes. Growth and higher-tier workflows expand into leads and sales so teams can keep service and revenue conversations connected.
How does the AI layer work?
Atlas AI and chat features help teams move faster with conversational workflows around tickets, clients, projects, leads, and sales tasks.
Does the calendar only show meetings?
No. RocketPSA can surface scheduled events alongside project and ticket due dates so operations teams can see work and time commitments together.
What about Microsoft Entra ID?
The education and tenant-aware authentication work in the main repo show support for Entra ID configuration at the tenant level.
Can schools use role-based access?
Yes. The central repo includes account and role management patterns for IT staff, administrators, and school-linked users.
Do you support custom integrations?
Yes. RocketPSA includes webview integrations and connected workflows for tools like QuickBooks, Google Drive, and identity providers.
